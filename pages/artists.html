Artist spotlight

Trauma

2025 - current

Trauma is a Bolivian American singer, producer, and contemporary artist known for an approach to electronic music that blends spoken word, heartfelt writing, and downbeat dialogue into something closer to a vocal diary.

His work stays experimental, subversive, melancholic, and aggressive at the same time. Also known for his soundtrack work, he continues pushing the line between modern electronic and experimental music while building across both NOVAGANG and L0%ER TABLE.
